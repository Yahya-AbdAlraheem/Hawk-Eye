Frequently Asked Questions

Find Quick Answers To Common Questions About Our Services And Tools.

What is OSINT and how do your tools use it?
OSINT stands for Open Source Intelligence. Our tools collect and analyze publicly available information from various sources including the dark web, Telegram channels, and domain registries to help you identify potential security threats and leaked information related to your digital footprint.
Are my searches and queries kept private?
Yes, privacy is our priority. All searches conducted through our platform are securely processed, and we do not store any sensitive user data on our servers. Your searches are private and are not shared with third parties.
How does the Password Leaked Lookup service work?
Our Password Leaked Lookup service securely compares the hash of your password against known breach databases like rockyou.txt without ever storing your actual password. This helps you determine if your password has been compromised in previous data breaches without exposing your credentials.
What should I do if I find my information has been leaked?
If you discover your information has been compromised, we recommend immediately changing your passwords, enabling two-factor authentication where possible, monitoring your accounts for suspicious activity, and contacting the relevant services to secure your accounts. Feel free to contact us for more specific guidance.
Can I request a custom OSINT solution for my organization?
Yes, we welcome collaboration opportunities and can discuss custom OSINT solutions tailored to your organization's specific needs. Please use our contact form and select "Collaboration" as the subject to start the conversation.
