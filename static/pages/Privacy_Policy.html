1. Introduction

Welcome to Hawk Eye's Privacy Policy. At Hawk Eye, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our OSINT (Open Source Intelligence) tools and services.

We understand the sensitive nature of cybersecurity and intelligence gathering. Our privacy practices are designed with this in mind, focusing on maintaining confidentiality while providing valuable security services. By using our website, tools, or services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

We prioritize your data security and operate with the highest standards of data protection and ethical handling.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you register, contact us, or use our services, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, and other contact details you provide.
  • Usage Information: We collect information about how you interact with our website and services, including IP addresses, browser type, pages visited, time spent, and features used.
  • Search Queries: The queries you enter into our tools are processed to deliver results, but we implement privacy-preserving measures to protect your search activities.
  • Technical Data: We collect device information, operating system details, and network information to enhance service performance and security.

Special Note on Password Lookups: Our Password Leaked Lookup service only processes secure hashes of passwords. We never store, log, or maintain your actual passwords in our systems.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve our OSINT tools and services
  • To process and respond to your inquiries and requests
  • To notify you about changes to our services or policies
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or security vulnerabilities
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize user experience
  • To personalize your experience when using our tools

We process your information based on legitimate interests, contract fulfillment, legal compliance, and with your consent where required by applicable law.

4. Data Security Measures

We implement robust security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • End-to-end encryption for data transmission
  • Secure hashing for password verification
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Access controls and authentication mechanisms
  • Server-side encryption for stored data
  • Regular security training for our team members

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. We continuously improve our security protocols to address emerging threats.

Our systems are built with security-first architecture, and we employ multiple layers of protection to safeguard your data.

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties except in the following cases:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website and services, conducting business, or servicing you, provided they agree to keep this information confidential.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information when required by law, to comply with a legal process, or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.
  • With Your Consent: We may share information with third parties when we have your explicit consent to do so.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, your personal information may be transferred as part of the transaction. We will notify you via email and/or a prominent notice on our website of any change in ownership or uses of your personal information.

Any third parties with whom we share information are bound by confidentiality agreements and are restricted from using the information for any purpose other than those outlined in this policy.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, for legitimate business operations, and to comply with legal obligations.

Specific retention periods include:

  • Account information is retained as long as your account is active
  • Search queries may be stored in anonymized form for up to 30 days
  • Contact form submissions are retained for up to one year
  • Usage logs are retained for 90 days for security and service improvement purposes

When personal information is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it to prevent unauthorized access.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section. We will respond to your request within 30 days.

We respect your privacy rights and are committed to providing you with control over your personal information.

8. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience, analyze site usage, and assist in our marketing efforts. Cookies are small files that a site or its service provider transfers to your device which enables the site to recognize your browser and remember certain information.

We use the following types of cookies:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly and cannot be disabled.
  • Analytical/Performance Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site.
  • Functionality Cookies: Allow the website to remember choices you make.
  • Targeting Cookies: Record your visit to our website, the pages you visit, and the links you follow.

You can control cookies through your browser settings, but disabling certain cookies may limit your ability to use some features of our site.
